I am a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at The Ohio State University, where I am advised by Prof. Ness B. Shroff. My research advances safe, trustworthy, and scalable AI.

Prior to joining OSU, I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Tehran.

My current research focuses on provably safe RL theory, distributed systems for multi-objective LLM alignment (RLHF), and deep RL methods for the Pre-Training and Post-Training of intelligent agents.
